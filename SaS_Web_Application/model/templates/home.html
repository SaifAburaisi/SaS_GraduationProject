What is Dendect?

Dendect is a web application that allows you to upload your dental x-ray and detect five common problems: Conservative(Fillings, Crowns), Bridge, Implant, and RCT. Our deep learning algorithm can analyze your x-ray and provide you with an accurate diagnosis.

How does it work?

To use Dendect, simply create an account, upload your x-ray, and wait for our algorithm to analyze the image.
